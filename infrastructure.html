Infrastructure

STATE OF THE ART
MANUFACTURING FACILITY,
SPREAD OVER AN AREA OF 4.5 LACS SQFT

Enquire Now

It is here that our products are manufactured, under the state-of-the-art machinery that conforms to the world-class quality standards and in an environment free of pollution.

The products that we manufacture are first procured in raw form from the agricultural fields. They are then processed under the high-end machines in various units in our factory: a unit that houses Buhler machines, a fumigation chamber, a large unit with drum-type roasters that are digitally manned and an integrated packaging unit.

During the multiple stages of processing, the raw spices are milled, sieved, cleaned and finely ground. Nonetheless, we ensure that they retain the aroma that is inherent in them, so that when you use them in your cooking, you will feel a rich aroma wafting through the air, inviting everyone in your house to taste it instantly.

Retail Packaging

When building a brand , retailers and importers prefer outsourcing their packaging operations . NANI offers a sector agnostic packaging and labeling unit which is also one the largest facilities in the country . Customers can choose from an array of packaging materials ( pillow pouches, mono cartons , boxes etc. ) or customise it to fit their requirements .

Private label packaging is one of the pillars on which NANI very proudly stands . For customers trying to enter niche spaces, they require packaging that is up to the standards of the certification boards . Therefore we collaborate with them in 2 ways

1. A customer may choose from our diversified portfolio of ready products to be rebranded and made suitable for their target audience.

2. Alternatively , a customer has the complete flexibility to create their own spice mix with the branding and packaging of their choice , to be manufactured and labelled under our roof .
